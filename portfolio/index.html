Grayson Earle Portfolio Links

  • Why don’t the cops fight each other? (2022)
    Video, 9:42
    A desktop documentary about an attempt to modify the relationships between police officers in the popular video game Grand Theft Auto V. The flexibility of the game world makes it possible to fire vehicles from weapons, teleport to any location, and modify the textures of billboards. The relationships between police officers in the game, however, are immutable. It is this unbreakable rule within the simulation of the urban environment that betrays the game’s deep sociopolitical assumptions.

  • EntropyOS (2022)
    Video Installation (Non-durational software-generated audiovisual environment)
    A live simulation about indeterminacy and contemporary computing. It speaks to issues surrounding encryption and the political economy of random numbers, using randomness to drive a narrative taking place on a computer desktop. The piece is infinite in duration, constructed in realtime along with a generative soundscape by Ron Shalom.
  • Usefulness of a Useless Neural Network (2021)
    This video charts the artist's production of a neural network which uses machine learning to do simple addition. This absurd and rather useless task becomes a way to demystify the black box operations of machine learning algorithms and explore what we imply when we talk about precision, accuracy, and optimization.
